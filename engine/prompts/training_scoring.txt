## Scoring Guidelines

**Overall Score Calculation:**
- 90-100: Excellent - Framework applied consistently and naturally
- 70-89: Good - Framework mostly applied with minor gaps
- 50-69: Developing - Framework partially applied, key elements missing
- Below 50: Needs Practice - Framework not effectively applied

**Framework Step Scoring:**
- Mark as "demonstrated": true if the learner clearly showed understanding and application
- Score reflects quality of application (0-100 scale)
- Provide specific evidence from the trace

**Coaching Tone:**
- Be encouraging but honest
- Focus on growth, not criticism
- Provide specific, actionable feedback
- Celebrate what they did well before suggesting improvements
